My name is Seth Wilkin, and under my creative projects I am known as Haltaar.

For the better part of the last decade I have been working on various projects, and on this site I wanted to showcase my music and photography.

This is a handpicked selection of my work. On my Soundcloud and Instagram profiles you can find a more complete collection of my creative output.

Music: To produce music I use a Windows PC running Ableton Live. I use an Akai EiE Pro interface to offload sound processing and enable recording of mulitple instruments. I use an M-Audio Oxygen keyboard and a selection of guitars and bass guitars. Almost all sound design is done with stock Ableton plugins, with the notable exception of Glitch/Glitch2 by dBlue/Illformed respectively, and Addictive Keys piano by XLN.

Photography: All of my photographs are taken using Android phones. Older pictures were taken using a Nexus 6P, which I recently upgraded to a Pixel XL. I use the stock Google Photos app for image processing and editing.
